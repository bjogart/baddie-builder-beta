Quick 'n dirty list of tags

  • [hp]/[dmg]

    • plus: increase effective HP/damage by amount
    • minus: decrease effective HP/damage by amount
    • times: multiply effective HP/damage by amount
    • divide: divide effective HP/damage by amount
    • dsize/dcount/dplus: modify dice output, may error if dice output is far from actual value
    • heal ([hp] only): removes HP tag so you can insert it for actions that heal.

  • [hit]

    • vs: for effect rolls/saving throws
    • plus/minus: as above

  • [ac], [str], [dex], [con], [int], [wis], [cha]

    • plus/minus: as above
    • right now plus/minus are how you change effect DCs/saving throws

  • [uses]

    • should always have some value; tracks how many 'uses' an entry uses compared to the total. Three entries with [uses: 1] take up exactly the allotted amount each; but if you take two entries with [uses: 2] and [uses: 1], then the former takes a share of the hit points/damage used by the latter. Use sparingly, and only for flashy but limited attacks (ie. 1/battle fireball). As a rule of thumb, limit yourself to 3 uses/actions for each creature (3 [uses: 1] actions; 1 [uses: 1] and 1 [uses: 2], et cetera).

  • [mv]

    • walk/climb/fly/swim: format appropriate speed (default speed is walk 30 ft.)
